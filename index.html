AfriMed Finance Society (AFS)

Building Sustainable Future in Africa

The African & Mediterranean Finance Society (AfriMed Finance Society or AFS) is an academic organization that aims at becoming a premier international forum for studying, communicating, encouraging, and celebrating the latest research in financial economics. AFS is particularly devoted to the promotion of knowledge and knowledge mobilization in Africa. AFS aims also at providing mutual association of academics, practitioners, and doctoral students with interest in researching relevant questions in the greater field of Finance to improve public understanding of financial economic problems. AFS encourages the study of finance in the African & Mediterranean economies and elsewhere in the world.

AFS Meeting

2022 AFS Summer Conference

The AfriMed Finance Society (AFS) is organizing its 2022 Summer Finance Conference designed to bring together leading scholars in the field. The conference will be held in the coastal city of Mahdia (Tunisia) from the 22nd to the 24th of July 2022. An optional guided day tour will be organized on July 24th. Please find more details in the AFS Call for Papers below.

Conference Site

The AFS 2022 Summer Conference will be held in The Iberostar Selection Royal El Mansour (Mahdia). Conference participants can stay also in neighboring hotels: Mahdia Palace Thalasso and Nour Palace Thalasso & Spa (Mahdia).

Call for Papers

The COVID-19 pandemic has had differential impacts on individuals, communities, and countries, stressing the importance of focusing on responsible or sustainable investments in the aftermath of the pandemic. Research is needed in informing purposefully designed efforts to stimulate “equity, resilience, sustainability and progress towards the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)”. In support of these efforts, the AFS invites finance papers that promote resilient post-pandemic reality. Papers at the interface of corporate finance, asset pricing, behavioral finance, financial intermediation, household finance, financial market microstructure and international finance are particularly welcome.

PAPER SELECTION: We tentatively aim to have 10 papers in the program. Presenters can expect quality discussants, an active audience, and many informal interactions with participants. Papers should be well developed, but at a stage where they can still benefit from the group’s discussion. Please submit an electronic version of the paper (MS Word or PDF format) no later than May 29, 2022 by uploading your paper at the conference website. Paper selection will be finalized by mid-June 2022. If you have difficulties uploading your paper or for any additional questions please contact the conference coordinator Mrs. Catherine Cunningham at info@afrimedfinance.com.

Submit Your Paper

Please submit your paper using the form or by emailing your paper to: info@afrimedfinance.com.
Please note that only authors of accepted papers will be contacted.
